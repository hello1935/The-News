IndianOil unveils India's first green hydrogen-run bus that emits just water


India's top oil firm IOC on September 25 unveiled the nation's first green hydrogen-powered
bus that emits just water as it takes
the lead in bringing out unrivaled tools to replace fossil fuels.

Indian Oil Corporation (IOC) will produce close to 75 kg of hydrogen by splitting water using electricity from renewable sources. This hydrogen will be used to power two buses which will ply across the national capital region for trial runs.
